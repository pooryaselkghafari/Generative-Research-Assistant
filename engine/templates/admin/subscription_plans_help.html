Method 1: Using the Button (Recommended)

  1. In the content editor, place your cursor where you want the subscription plans to appear
  2. Click the "Insert Subscription Plans" button in the toolbar (look for the price tag icon)
  3. The subscription plans will automatically be inserted and will update whenever you change plans in the admin

Method 2: Manual Insertion

If the button doesn't appear, you can manually add this code in the editor (switch to "Source" view):

{% load subscription_tags %}
{% subscription_plans %}

Customization Options:

  • To change which plan is "featured" (highlighted), use: {% subscription_plans featured_plan_index=3 %}
  • The plans will automatically display all active subscription plans from your database
  • Features, prices, and limits are pulled directly from your SubscriptionPlan and SubscriptionTierSettings models
