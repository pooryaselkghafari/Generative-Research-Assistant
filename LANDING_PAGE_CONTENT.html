Why Generative Research Assistant?

The problem we solve and the solution we provide

One tiny mistake in your code can invalidate entire research papers. We've seen it happen too many times in social science research, and it happened to us too.

That's why we spent years building custom libraries in R and Python to automate a big part of statistical analysis. Now, we've put all these libraries into a clean, intuitive UI - and Generative Research Assistant was born.

The analysis part is completely algorithmic - there's zero chance for hallucination and errors. Every calculation is transparent, reproducible, and verifiable.
